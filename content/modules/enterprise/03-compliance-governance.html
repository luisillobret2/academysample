Enterprise Compliance Landscape

Enterprise organizations must navigate a complex web of regulatory requirements, industry standards, and internal policies. Mend.io helps automate compliance evidence generation and continuous monitoring.

💳

SOC 2 Type II

Trust Service Criteria covering security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy.

🏥

ISO 27001

Information security management system (ISMS) with Annex A controls covering software development security.

💳

PCI DSS 4.0

Payment card industry requirements for secure software development, vulnerability management, and access control.

🌎

FedRAMP / NIST 800-53

US government security framework requiring continuous monitoring, vulnerability management, and supply chain risk management.

Governance Policies at Scale

Enterprise governance requires consistent policy enforcement across hundreds or thousands of repositories:

  • Centralized Policy Management — Define policies once, enforce everywhere with inheritance
  • Exception Workflows — Structured processes for temporary policy exceptions with approval chains
  • Drift Detection — Alert when repositories fall out of compliance with organizational policies
  • Audit Trail — Complete history of policy changes, exceptions, and enforcement decisions
  • Role-Based Access — Granular permissions for who can view, modify, and override policies

Executive Reporting & Dashboards

Security leadership needs high-level visibility into the organization's security posture without drowning in technical details:

  • Risk Score Trends — Organization-wide risk score over time, broken down by business unit
  • SLA Compliance — Percentage of vulnerabilities remediated within defined SLAs
  • Coverage Metrics — What percentage of repositories are actively scanned?
  • Top Risks — The highest-impact vulnerabilities across the organization
  • Team Benchmarks — Compare security performance across teams and business units
