The Business Case for Application Security

Application security is no longer a technical checkbox — it's a business imperative. The average cost of a data breach reached $4.45 million in 2023, with breaches involving software supply chain compromises costing even more. For partner organizations, building a strong AppSec practice creates competitive differentiation and new revenue opportunities.

▶

Why AppSec is a Business Priority

5:30

Market Drivers

  • Regulatory Pressure — EO 14028, EU CRA, SEC disclosure rules, PCI DSS 4.0, and DORA are creating mandatory security requirements
  • Customer Demand — Enterprise buyers increasingly require security attestations, SBOMs, and proof of secure development practices from vendors
  • Insurance Requirements — Cyber insurance carriers now evaluate software security practices as part of underwriting
  • Board-Level Attention — CISOs now report to boards regularly; AppSec budgets are growing 15-20% annually

💡 Partner Opportunity

Organizations that cannot build internal AppSec capabilities will seek partners. The global application security market is projected to reach $25 billion by 2029. Partners certified on Mend.io are positioned to capture this demand.

ROI Framework

Use this framework to build a business case for Mend.io with prospects:
