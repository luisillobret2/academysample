Scaling Your Mend Practice

Growing a successful Mend partner practice requires strategic investment in people, processes, and go-to-market activities. This module covers proven strategies for scaling from initial partnership to a thriving security practice.

Growth Levers

👥

Invest in certifications, hire AppSec specialists, and build a dedicated Mend practice team.

Develop high-margin services: assessments, implementation, managed security, and training.

Co-marketing with Mend, industry events, thought leadership, and customer reference programs.

🏆

Progress from Registered to Platinum through revenue growth, certifications, and customer satisfaction.

Service Portfolio Development

The highest-margin revenue comes from professional services wrapped around the Mend platform:

  • Security Assessment ($15-30K) — Evaluate customer's current AppSec maturity, identify gaps, recommend Mend deployment strategy
  • Implementation Services ($25-75K) — Deploy Mend across the customer's environment, configure policies, integrate with CI/CD
  • Developer Training ($10-25K) — Custom training programs for development teams on secure coding and Mend usage
  • Managed AppSec ($5-15K/month) — Ongoing vulnerability management, policy tuning, and security reporting as a service
  • Compliance Automation ($20-50K) — Configure Mend for SOC 2, ISO 27001, or PCI DSS compliance evidence generation

Go-to-Market Strategies

Effective GTM for a Mend practice combines inbound and outbound approaches:

Inbound Marketing

  • Publish AppSec thought leadership (blogs, whitepapers, webinars)
  • Create Mend-specific case studies showcasing customer outcomes
  • Host local security meetups and workshops
  • Develop a free "AppSec Maturity Assessment" tool to generate leads

Outbound Sales

  • Target companies with recent security incidents or regulatory changes
  • Partner with Mend's sales team on joint accounts
  • Attend security conferences (RSA, Black Hat, BSides) with Mend
  • Leverage Mend Market Development Funds (MDF) for campaigns

Measuring Practice Health

Track these KPIs to measure and grow your Mend practice:

  • Recurring Revenue — Annual recurring Mend license + managed services revenue
  • Services Attach Rate — Percentage of Mend deals that include professional services
  • Customer Retention — Year-over-year renewal rate for your Mend customers
  • Net Revenue Retention — Revenue from existing customers vs. prior year (target: 120%+)
  • Certifications per FTE — Average Mend certifications per team member
  • Pipeline Generation — New qualified opportunities created per quarter

💡 Growth Tip

The most successful Mend partners achieve a 2:1 or higher services-to-license ratio. For every $1 of Mend license sold, target $2+ in professional services revenue.
