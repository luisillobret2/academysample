🕐 35 min 📚 4 sections ⭐ 200 XP Executive

Practice Development Framework

Building a successful application security practice — whether as a consulting firm, MSSP, or internal team — requires a structured approach that balances technical depth with business scalability. This module provides a framework for practice leaders who want to build a profitable, repeatable AppSec offering around Mend.io.

People

Hire and train certified consultants. Invest in Mend certifications. Build a talent pipeline through university partnerships.

🛠️

Process

Develop standardized delivery methodologies: assessment frameworks, implementation playbooks, and customer success runbooks.

💻

Go-to-Market

Package offerings into repeatable service SKUs. Develop case studies, ROI models, and competitive positioning.

Managed AppSec services — where the partner operates Mend on behalf of the customer, triaging alerts, managing policies, and producing compliance reports — generate the highest recurring revenue and longest customer retention.

Scaling Your Practice

  • Certification Targets — Aim for at least 2 certified Mend professionals per 10 customer accounts. Higher certification density correlates with faster deal closure and higher customer satisfaction
  • Delivery Accelerators — Build reusable Terraform modules, CI/CD templates, and policy-as-code libraries that reduce implementation time from weeks to days
  • Customer Success Metrics — Track time-to-value (first scan), adoption rate (% of repos onboarded), and risk reduction (vulnerability backlog trend)
  • Co-Selling with Mend — Leverage Mend's partner deal registration for margin protection and joint account planning for strategic accounts
  • Thought Leadership — Publish security research, speak at conferences, and contribute to open-source projects to build market credibility

Partner Tier Progression

Mend's partner program rewards investment in the practice:

  • Registered — Entry level: access to deal registration and basic training
  • Silver — 2+ certified practitioners, 3+ customer implementations
  • Gold — 5+ certified practitioners, dedicated partner manager, co-marketing funds
  • Platinum — 10+ certified practitioners, joint solution development, executive sponsorship
