POC & Trial Management

🕒 40 min 📚 4 sections ⭐ 175 XP Level 2 - Intermediate

🎯 Learning Objectives

  • Scope a POC with measurable, time-boxed success criteria
  • Plan the technical setup and integration steps
  • Manage the POC to a decision, not an open-ended trial
  • Deliver an executive readout that maps results to business value

Define Success Criteria First

A POC without exit criteria becomes a free trial that never closes. Agree the finish line before you start.

CriterionExample target
Noise reduction≥ 60% fewer actionable findings via reachability
Time to first fixAuto-remediation PR merged within 1 day
CoverageAll target repos and languages scanned
IntegrationWired into the customer’s CI and SCM

Run the POC

  1. Kickoff — confirm scope, criteria, stakeholders, and a 2-3 week timeline.
  2. Setup — connect repos and CI/CD; enable reachability and policies.
  3. Checkpoints — hold twice-weekly syncs to keep momentum and remove blockers.
  4. Readout — present results against the agreed criteria and ask for the decision.

💡 Time-Box Everything

Set a hard end date at kickoff. Momentum is your ally; an open-ended POC loses executive attention.

The Executive Readout

Translate technical wins into business outcomes the economic buyer cares about.

⚠️ Don’t Bury the Ask

End the readout with a clear next step: proposed license, start date, and deal registration.
