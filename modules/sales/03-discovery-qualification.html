Discovery & Qualification

🕒 35 min 📚 4 sections ⭐ 150 XP Level 2 - Intermediate

🎯 Learning Objectives

  • Recognize the Mend.io ideal customer profile (ICP) and disqualify early
  • Ask layered discovery questions that surface pain and business impact
  • Qualify opportunities using the MEDDICC framework
  • Map stakeholders and identify your champion and economic buyer

The Ideal Customer Profile

Strong Mend.io fits share a few signals. Use them to prioritize your pipeline and to disqualify quickly when the fit is weak.

  • 50+ developers and an active CI/CD pipeline
  • Meaningful open-source and container footprint
  • A compliance driver (SOC 2, PCI, FedRAMP, EU CRA) or a recent security incident
  • An existing scanner generating too much noise or too little remediation
  • Emerging AI/LLM adoption with no governance in place

Discovery Questions That Work

Move from surface symptoms to quantified business impact. Layer your questions.

  1. Situation — “Walk me through how a vulnerability gets from detection to a merged fix today.”
  2. Problem — “How much of your backlog is noise your team can safely ignore?”
  3. Impact — “What does a missed critical or a failed audit cost you in time and dollars?”
  4. Vision — “If fixes shipped automatically as PRs, what would that free your team to do?”

💡 Listen for Metrics

Capture the customer’s own numbers (open findings, MTTR, audit hours). You will reuse them in the business case and the proposal.
