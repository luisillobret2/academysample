Deal Registration Process

Deal registration protects your investment in developing an opportunity and ensures you receive proper credit and margin protection. Registered deals receive priority support, better pricing, and conflict resolution protection.

Registration Requirements

  • Customer Information — Company name, industry, size, and primary contact details
  • Opportunity Details — Estimated deal size, timeline, competitive landscape, and current security tools
  • Partner Value-Add — What services and value are you bringing to the deal?
  • Technical Requirements — Technology stack, number of developers, repositories, and deployment model

Deal Registration Benefits

💰

Margin Protection

Registered deals receive guaranteed minimum margins, protecting your investment in the sales process.

👥

Conflict Resolution

First-to-register wins. If another partner registers the same opportunity, the original registration takes priority.

🚀

Sales Support

Access to Mend sales engineers, pre-sales support, and executive sponsorship for large opportunities.

🏆

Incentive Eligibility

Only registered deals qualify for quarterly SPIFs, annual bonuses, and partner tier advancement credit.

Pipeline Management Best Practices

A healthy pipeline is the foundation of predictable revenue. Follow these best practices to maintain pipeline quality:

  • 3x Coverage — Maintain pipeline at 3x your quarterly target for predictable attainment
  • Stage Hygiene — Move deals through stages accurately; don't inflate stage to show false progress
  • Close Date Accuracy — Update close dates based on actual customer commitment, not wishful thinking
  • Multi-Threading — Engage multiple stakeholders (security, engineering, procurement) to de-risk deals
  • Regular Reviews — Weekly pipeline reviews with your Mend channel manager keep deals on track

Deal Qualification (MEDDPICC)

  • Metrics — What business outcomes will success be measured by?
  • Economic Buyer — Who has budget authority?
  • Decision Criteria — What factors will drive the purchase decision?
  • Decision Process — What is the approval workflow?
  • Paper Process — What procurement/legal steps are required?
  • Identified Pain — What specific problems need solving?
  • Champion — Who is your internal advocate?
  • Competition — Who else is being evaluated?

Partner Tiers & Incentives

Mend's partner program rewards consistent performance with increasing benefits:

  • Registered Partner — Basic deal registration, standard margins, portal access
  • Silver Partner — Enhanced margins, 2 NFR licenses, quarterly business reviews
  • Gold Partner — Premium margins, dedicated channel manager, MDF funds, co-marketing
  • Platinum Partner — Highest margins, executive sponsor, joint go-to-market, event sponsorship

💡 Advancement Tip

Partner tier is evaluated annually based on revenue, certifications, and customer satisfaction. Focus on getting your first 3 deals closed and 2 team members certified to reach Silver tier quickly.
