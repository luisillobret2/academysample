Positioning Summary

💡 The One-Liner

Endor Labs is a fast-growing challenger with excellent function-level reachability for SCA noise reduction. However, it is SCA-first with newer SAST, no AI security layer, limited license governance, and no equivalent to Renovate's automated dependency management. Lead with platform breadth (SAST + SCA + AI security + Renovate), production-ready AI hardening, and 1.7B Renovate pulls powering upgrade paths.

Why Teams Switch to Mend.io

Platform breadth Endor cannot match

Mend.io unifies SAST, SCA, AI security, secrets, container, and Renovate in one platform. Endor Labs is SCA-first with newer SAST and no AI security layer, forcing teams to buy additional tools.

AI security production-ready today

Mend AI delivers continuous AI inventory, AI BoM, prompt hardening, and red teaming, all GA. Endor Labs has no AI security offering at all.

Renovate drives continuous dependency health

1.7B Renovate installs and Merge Confidence power cadence-driven upgrade PRs. Endor's fix PRs are security-triggered only, so most teams still need Renovate or Dependabot alongside it.

Mature SAST vs. a 2025 launch

Mend SAST scans 10x faster with +38% precision and +48% recall across 200+ languages. Endor's SAST launched in August 2025 and is still building language coverage and rule depth.

License governance that goes deep

Mend.io detects license conflicts at the file level and provides legal insights. Endor Labs lists licenses but lacks conflict analysis and legal guidance.

Simpler pricing, no add-on stacking

Mend.io offers transparent pricing with no scan limits. Endor Labs uses quote-based tiered pricing with separate add-ons for SAST (CoDe), Patches, and SBOM Hub.

Endor Labs Background

🔎 At a Glance

Founded in 2022 by Varun Badhwar (CEO) and Dimitri Stiliadis (CTO), both ex-Palo Alto Networks / Prisma Cloud. Headquartered in Palo Alto. Raised $25M seed (2022), $70M Series A (2023), and $93M Series B (2025). Named customers include OpenAI, Cursor, Snowflake, Netskope, and Atlassian. Commercial only, no free tier.

⚠️ Where Endor Wins

Endor Labs has genuinely strong function-level reachability across 40+ languages, which can significantly reduce SCA alert noise for teams with large dependency trees. Their Endor Patches (backported fixes for Java and Python) are a unique differentiator for critical findings with no upstream fix. Acknowledge this strength and pivot to platform breadth, AI security, and Renovate.

Objection-Handling Reminder

⚠️ Keep It Classy

Never disparage a competitor by name in front of a customer. Contrast on outcomes (platform breadth, AI security, time-to-fix, total cost of ownership) and let the buyer draw their own conclusion. Acknowledge Endor's reachability strength and pivot to what they cannot do.
