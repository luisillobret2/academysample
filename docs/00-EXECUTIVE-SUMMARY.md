# Mend Learn: Partner Learning Platform - Executive Summary

## Vision

**Mend Learn** is a world-class partner enablement platform designed to accelerate partner activation, drive certifications, and generate measurable pipeline and revenue through structured learning experiences. Modeled after the best-in-class patterns from Snyk Learn, AWS Skill Builder, and Microsoft Learn, Mend Learn is purpose-built for the Mend.io partner ecosystem.

## Strategic Objectives

| Objective | How Mend Learn Delivers |
|---|---|
| Partner Activation | Guided onboarding journeys with role-specific fast-tracks |
| Technical Certifications | Six-tier certification program with proctored exams and digital badges |
| Sales Competency | Battlecard-driven sales tracks, objection-handling simulations, pitch labs |
| Deal Registrations | Allbound-integrated incentives tied to certification completion |
| Pipeline Generation | Certification unlocks co-marketing funds, lead sharing, and deal reg bonuses |
| Partner-Sourced Revenue | Revenue attribution dashboard correlating certifications to closed deals |
| Product Adoption | Hands-on labs and sandbox environments that mirror real customer deployments |
| Partner Engagement | Gamification engine with XP, leaderboards, quarterly challenges, and rewards |

## Platform Pillars

1. **Role-Based Learning Paths** - Progressive Beginner to Expert tracks tailored per persona
2. **Certification Program** - Six tiers from Associate to Master Architect
3. **Hands-On Labs** - Browser-based sandbox environments with real Mend.io instances
4. **Gamification Engine** - Points, XP, badges, leaderboards, and partner rankings
5. **AI-Powered Coaching** - Personal learning coach, objection-handling roleplay, certification prep
6. **Allbound Integration** - SSO, badge sync, certification status, deal registration incentives
7. **Analytics Dashboard** - Revenue attribution, certification ROI, partner engagement metrics

## Key Differentiators vs. Competitors

- **Revenue-linked learning**: Every certification maps to a measurable pipeline and revenue outcome
- **Partner-first design**: Unlike vendor training portals, Mend Learn is built around partner business models (VAR margins, MSSP recurring revenue, GSI practice building)
- **AI roleplay**: Sales pitch practice and objection handling powered by AI, not just static content
- **Sandbox labs**: Real Mend.io environments, not just videos or slides
- **Allbound-native**: Deep integration with the existing partner portal, not a separate island
