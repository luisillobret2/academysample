# 4. Certifications

## Design Philosophy

The certification program follows a **dual-track model**: a general competency ladder (Associate → Professional → Expert) and two role-specific specialist tracks (Technical Specialist, Sales Specialist) plus a capstone (Master Architect). This mirrors Cisco's CCNA/CCNP/CCIE + specialist model, which is the most successful partner certification program in enterprise tech.

**WHY dual-track**: A single linear path forces sales reps through technical content and engineers through sales content. Dual tracks respect role boundaries while maintaining a common foundation. Cisco and Palo Alto both report that dual-track programs achieve 3x higher certification rates than single-track alternatives.

## Certification 1: Mend.io Certified Associate

### Overview

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Target Audience** | All partner roles - the universal entry point |
| **Prerequisites** | Level 1 Foundation Track completion |
| **Estimated Study Time** | 4-6 hours (including foundation track) |
| **Exam Duration** | 60 minutes |
| **Exam Format** | 50 multiple-choice and multiple-select questions |
| **Passing Score** | 70% (35/50) |
| **Delivery** | Online, unproctored, single attempt per 7-day period |
| **Badge** | Mend.io Certified Associate (digital badge via Credly) |
| **Renewal** | Every 12 months; 30-minute renewal exam or 10 CEU credits |
| **Cost** | Free for registered partners |

### Learning Objectives

Upon passing, the candidate can:

1. Explain the application security threat landscape and why software supply chain security matters
2. Describe Mend.io's product portfolio (SCA, SAST, Container, Secrets, Supply Chain) and key differentiators
3. Identify the ideal customer profile for Mend.io solutions
4. Articulate Mend.io's competitive positioning against Snyk, Veracode, and Checkmarx
5. Navigate the Mend.io platform and understand core workflows
6. Describe Mend.io's deployment models and integration ecosystem

### Exam Blueprint

| Domain | Weight | Topics |
|---|---|---|
| AppSec Fundamentals | 20% | OWASP Top 10, supply chain risks, vulnerability types |
| Mend.io Product Knowledge | 35% | Product features, use cases, architecture |
| Market & Competitive | 20% | ICP, competitive positioning, value proposition |
| Platform Navigation | 15% | UI workflows, basic configuration, reporting |
| Partner Program | 10% | Partner tiers, benefits, deal registration |

**WHY free and unproctored**: The Associate cert is a volume play - every partner should have it. Barriers (cost, proctoring) reduce adoption. Snyk's free certifications drove 10x more certifications than Veracode's paid model. The 7-day retry window prevents brute-forcing without creating frustration.

## Certification 2: Mend.io Certified Professional

### Overview

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Target Audience** | SEs, Admins, Security Consultants with hands-on Mend.io experience |
| **Prerequisites** | Associate certification + at least 2 Level 2 product tracks completed |
| **Estimated Study Time** | 15-20 hours (including product tracks) |
| **Exam Duration** | 90 minutes |
| **Exam Format** | 60 questions: 40 multiple-choice + 10 scenario-based + 10 drag-and-drop |
| **Passing Score** | 75% (45/60) |
| **Delivery** | Online, proctored (webcam + screen share via PSI or Examity) |
| **Badge** | Mend.io Certified Professional (digital badge via Credly) |
| **Renewal** | Every 18 months; 45-minute renewal exam or 20 CEU credits |
| **Cost** | Free for partners at Silver tier and above; $150 for others |

### Learning Objectives

Upon passing, the candidate can:

1. Configure and manage Mend.io for SCA, SAST, and at least one additional product
2. Design and implement scanning policies across an organization
3. Integrate Mend.io into CI/CD pipelines using multiple methods
4. Interpret scan results, prioritize vulnerabilities, and recommend remediation
5. Configure SSO, RBAC, and organizational hierarchies
6. Generate and interpret compliance and executive reports
7. Troubleshoot common deployment and scanning issues

### Exam Blueprint

| Domain | Weight | Topics |
|---|---|---|
| Product Configuration | 25% | Setup, policies, organization structure |
| Integration & Automation | 25% | CI/CD, SCM, IDE, API |
| Vulnerability Management | 20% | Triage, prioritization, remediation workflows |
| Administration | 15% | SSO, RBAC, monitoring, troubleshooting |
| Reporting & Compliance | 15% | Dashboards, exports, compliance frameworks |

**WHY proctored**: Professional certification carries market credibility. Proctoring ensures integrity and makes the credential meaningful on resumes and in customer conversations. Every major vendor (AWS, Azure, Cisco) proctors their mid-tier and above certifications.

## Certification 3: Mend.io Certified Expert

### Overview

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Target Audience** | Senior SEs, Architects, and Consultants designing enterprise deployments |
| **Prerequisites** | Professional certification + Level 3 Expert track completion |
| **Estimated Study Time** | 25-35 hours (including expert tracks) |
| **Exam Duration** | 120 minutes |
| **Exam Format** | 40 questions + 2 lab-based practical scenarios (30 min each) |
| **Passing Score** | 80% overall (must pass both knowledge and practical sections) |
| **Delivery** | Online, proctored, with live lab environment |
| **Badge** | Mend.io Certified Expert (digital badge via Credly) |
| **Renewal** | Every 24 months; practical assessment or 30 CEU credits + contribution |
| **Cost** | Free for partners at Gold tier and above; $250 for others |

### Learning Objectives

Upon passing, the candidate can:

1. Design multi-tenant enterprise architectures for Mend.io
2. Implement advanced CI/CD integration patterns including GitOps
3. Build custom automation using the Mend.io API
4. Design governance and compliance frameworks using Mend.io
5. Plan and execute migrations from competitor products
6. Optimize Mend.io performance for large-scale deployments
7. Lead customer technical workshops and architecture reviews

### Exam Blueprint

| Domain | Weight | Topics |
|---|---|---|
| Enterprise Architecture | 25% | Multi-tenant design, HA/DR, performance |
| Advanced Integration | 25% | API, automation, custom workflows, multi-tool |
| Governance & Compliance | 20% | Frameworks, policy-as-code, audit |
| Migration & Optimization | 15% | Competitor migration, tuning, scaling |
| Practical Labs | 15% | Two real-world scenarios in live environment |

**WHY lab-based practical**: Expert-level credibility requires demonstrated ability, not just knowledge. AWS Solutions Architect Professional includes scenario-based questions; Google Cloud Professional Architect includes case studies. Lab-based practicals are the gold standard for proving hands-on expertise.

## Certification 4: Partner Technical Specialist

### Overview

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Target Audience** | Partner SEs and architects who lead technical evaluations |
| **Prerequisites** | Professional certification + Technical/SE Track + at least 3 product deep-dives |
| **Estimated Study Time** | 30-40 hours total |
| **Exam Duration** | 150 minutes |
| **Exam Format** | 30 knowledge questions + 3 practical labs + 1 demo recording |
| **Passing Score** | 80% knowledge + "Pass" on all 3 labs + "Pass" on demo |
| **Delivery** | Knowledge: proctored online. Labs: self-paced (7-day window). Demo: recorded and reviewed |
| **Badge** | Mend.io Partner Technical Specialist (premium badge via Credly) |
| **Renewal** | Every 24 months; updated demo recording + 2 customer reference letters |
| **Cost** | Free for all partners |

### Learning Objectives

Upon passing, the candidate can:

1. Deliver the complete standard Mend.io demo for SCA and SAST
2. Design and execute a customer POC with defined success criteria
3. Architect Mend.io solutions for enterprise customers
4. Handle technical objections from security teams and engineering leaders
5. Integrate Mend.io with the customer's specific toolchain
6. Lead a customer security workshop and produce recommendations

### Exam Components

| Component | Weight | Format |
|---|---|---|
| Knowledge Assessment | 30% | 30 multiple-choice questions (60 min) |
| Lab 1: Deployment & Config | 20% | Deploy and configure Mend.io from scratch (30 min) |
| Lab 2: Integration Challenge | 20% | Integrate with provided CI/CD environment (30 min) |
| Lab 3: Troubleshooting | 15% | Diagnose and fix 3 issues in a broken setup (30 min) |
| Demo Recording | 15% | Record 15-minute demo, graded by Mend.io SE team |

**WHY include a demo recording**: The ultimate test of an SE is delivering a compelling demo. No written exam measures this. Having Mend.io SEs review partner demos ensures quality and creates a feedback loop. Cisco's SE certification similarly includes a presentation component.

## Certification 5: Partner Sales Specialist

### Overview

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Target Audience** | Partner sales reps who actively sell Mend.io |
| **Prerequisites** | Associate certification + Sales Track completion |
| **Estimated Study Time** | 10-15 hours total |
| **Exam Duration** | 75 minutes |
| **Exam Format** | 40 questions: 25 multiple-choice + 10 scenario-based + 5 roleplay evaluations |
| **Passing Score** | 75% (30/40) |
| **Delivery** | Online, unproctored for MC; AI-evaluated for roleplay |
| **Badge** | Mend.io Partner Sales Specialist (digital badge via Credly) |
| **Renewal** | Every 12 months; updated competitive assessment + 5 CEU credits |
| **Cost** | Free for all partners |

### Learning Objectives

Upon passing, the candidate can:

1. Articulate Mend.io's value proposition to C-level and VP audiences
2. Conduct effective discovery calls using the Mend.io framework
3. Position Mend.io competitively against all major competitors
4. Handle the top 15 sales objections effectively
5. Navigate Mend.io's pricing model and build proposals
6. Register deals and leverage partner program benefits
7. Identify cross-sell and upsell opportunities

### Exam Components

| Component | Weight | Format |
|---|---|---|
| Product & Market Knowledge | 30% | Multiple-choice questions |
| Scenario-Based Selling | 30% | "What would you do?" scenarios |
| Competitive Positioning | 15% | Battlecard-based questions |
| AI Roleplay Evaluation | 25% | 5 objection-handling scenarios graded by AI |

**WHY AI-graded roleplay in the exam**: Sales is a performance skill. Multiple-choice alone doesn't validate that someone can actually deliver a pitch. AI roleplay evaluation checks for key messaging, objection response quality, and value articulation. This is the most innovative component of the certification program.

## Certification 6: Mend.io Master Architect

### Overview

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Target Audience** | Elite partner architects and consultants (< 50 globally) |
| **Prerequisites** | Expert certification + Partner Technical Specialist + 3 verified customer deployments |
| **Estimated Study Time** | 50+ hours (includes real-world experience) |
| **Exam Duration** | 4 hours (split across 2 sessions) |
| **Exam Format** | Design challenge (written architecture) + Defense (live presentation to Mend.io panel) |
| **Passing Score** | "Pass" by consensus of 3-person review panel |
| **Delivery** | Session 1: Written design (3 hrs, take-home, 7-day window). Session 2: Live defense (1 hr, video call) |
| **Badge** | Mend.io Master Architect (exclusive badge via Credly + physical trophy) |
| **Renewal** | Every 24 months; community contribution requirement + 1 case study publication |
| **Cost** | Free (by invitation or application only) |

### Learning Objectives

Upon passing, the candidate can:

1. Design enterprise-scale Mend.io architectures for Global 2000 companies
2. Lead cross-functional security transformation programs
3. Advise on organizational AppSec strategy and maturity improvement
4. Create reference architectures for specific industries (financial services, healthcare, government)
5. Mentor other partners and contribute to the Mend.io community
6. Represent Mend.io at industry events and customer advisory boards

### Exam Components

| Component | Format | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Design Challenge | Written (take-home) | Given a complex customer scenario (10,000+ developers, multi-cloud, regulated industry), produce a complete architecture: deployment design, integration strategy, governance model, migration plan, success metrics |
| Architecture Defense | Live presentation | Present and defend the design to a panel of Mend.io architects. Panel asks probing questions about trade-offs, alternatives, and edge cases |
| Customer References | Verification | 3 verified customer deployments with documented outcomes |

**WHY invitation-only with a defense**: The Master Architect is an elite credential that must carry extreme credibility. AWS's Principal Solutions Architect and Cisco's CCIE both maintain exclusivity through rigorous practical assessments. The defense format tests communication and critical thinking that written exams cannot. The physical trophy creates emotional attachment to the brand. Limiting to ~50 globally creates aspirational value.

**WHY Credly**: Credly is the industry standard for digital credentials. 95% of enterprise tech vendors use it. Partners can share badges on LinkedIn with one click, driving organic awareness. Credly's verification API also enables Allbound badge sync.

## Continuing Education Units (CEUs)

| Activity | CEUs Earned |
|---|---|
| Complete a new learning module | 1-3 CEU (based on duration) |
| Attend a Mend.io webinar | 2 CEU |
| Attend partner conference session | 3 CEU |
| Publish a blog post or case study | 5 CEU |
| Deliver a customer presentation | 3 CEU |
| Mentor another partner | 5 CEU |
| Complete a hands-on lab | 2 CEU |

**WHY CEUs for renewal**: Renewal exams alone create anxiety and drop-off. CEUs allow partners to stay certified through ongoing professional development. AWS and Microsoft both offer CEU-based renewal paths. The activities chosen here also benefit Mend.io (community content, mentoring, events).
